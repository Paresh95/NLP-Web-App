This website aims to provide an overview of some simple Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to analyse text. Hopefully, it’ll give you a flavour of NLP and maybe even spark an interest!

What is NLP?

NLP is a branch of artificial intelligence that allows computers to interpret and extract insights from human language. This draws from methods in linguistics, computer science and mathematics. The field of NLP has grown rapidly in recent year with an ever-increasing volume of textual data and with improvements in NLP techniques. Popular applications include monitoring brand sentiment, summarising news articles and analysing social media posts.
